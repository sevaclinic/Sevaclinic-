Refund & Cancellation Policy

This Refund and Cancellation Policy explains how you can cancel an order or request a refund for any product or service purchased through our Platform.

Order Cancellation

  • Cancellations are allowed only within 2 days of placing the order.
  • If the order has already been processed, shipped, or is out for delivery, cancellation may not be possible.
  • You may still reject the product at delivery if cancellation is not available.

Non-Cancellable Products

Items such as perishables (flowers, eatables, etc.) cannot be cancelled. A refund/replacement is allowed only if the product is proven to be of poor quality at delivery.

Damaged or Defective Items

  • Please report damaged/defective items within 2 days of receiving the product.
  • The seller will verify the issue before processing a return or replacement.
  • If the delivered product does not match the listing, report it within 2 days.

Manufacturer Warranty Items

For items covered by manufacturer warranty, please contact the manufacturer directly.

Refund Timeline

For approved refunds, BIBEKANANDA PAL will process the refund within 7 days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method unless stated otherwise.
